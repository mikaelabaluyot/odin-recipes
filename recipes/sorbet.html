Sorbet

Description

Make a healthy, no-added-sugar sorbet in mere minutes with this ingenious recipe! Use banana, strawberry, or a mixture of both! Get creative and use other frozen fruits, too

Ingredients

  • 12 fresh strawberries, hulled and chopped
  • 5 bananas, peeled and chopped

Steps

  1. Place strawberries and bananas in separate containers or resealable plastic bags; freeze at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Place strawberries in a food processor or blender and process until smooth.
  3. Place bananas in a food processor or blender and process until smooth.
