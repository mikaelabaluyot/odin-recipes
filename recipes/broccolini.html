Lemon Garlic Baby Broccoli

Description

Perfectly crisp-tender saut√©ed baby broccoli is a quick and easy side dish that pairs well with any protein

Ingredients

  • 2 baby broccoli, stem ends trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 dl water, or as needed
  • 1/2 lemon
  • salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Steps

  1. Rinse baby broccoli and pat dry.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Saute baby broccoli for 3 to 4 minutes, turning occasionally, until baby broccoli begins turning a bright green color and edges are starting to brown. Add garlic slices and stir until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Pour in water, cover with a lid and reduce heat to medium-low. Steam broccoli until crisp-tender and water has evaporated, 3 to 4 more minutes. If you prefer more tender baby broccoli, add in a bit more water, cover, and cook for a few more minutes
  3. Squeeze lemon over baby broccoli and season with salt to taste.
