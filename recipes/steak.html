Perfect Iron Steak

Description

This delicious flat iron steak was created from a combination of different recipes that I read.

Ingredients

  • 1 flat iron steak
  • 2 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh parsley
  • ¼ teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • ½ teaspoon chopped fresh chives
  • 1 dl Cabernet Sauvignon (or other dry red wine)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon dry mustard powder
